## About My Work & Repositories

If you are a hiring manager or recruiter, you might notice that my public commit history and open-source contributions are limited. 

For the past several years, I have been working in highly regulated, enterprise environments building advanced financial models and quantitative risk solutions. The vast majority of my work involves highly confidential, proprietary data, and heavily regulated algorithms. Due to strict NDAs and security protocols, I cannot publicly share the source code, pipelines, or data related to the proprietary models I have developed for my employer.

However, the lack of public code is reflective of the sensitive and high-impact nature of the projects I lead. My day-to-day technical expertise heavily involves:

*   **Languages, Big Data & Cloud Architecture:** Python, PySpark, SQL, Hadoop, and AWS (Amazon S3, Sagemaker) for large-scale data manipulation and infrastructure design.
*   **Machine Learning & Advanced Modeling:** Developing, validating, and deploying robust predictive models using frameworks like XGBoost, GBM, GLM, H2O AutoML, and stacked ensembles.
*   **Specialized Quantitative Analytics:** Translating complex business and risk requirements into deployed mathematical models, utilizing time-series analysis, discrete-time hazard modeling, and monotonically-constrained gradient boosting.
*   **Applied AI & GenAI Workflows:** Pioneering LLM-assisted development and AI agent methodologies (Claude, ChatGPT) to automate processes, engineer ground-truth datasets, and accelerate team-wide execution speed.
*   **Technical Leadership:** Directing ML strategy and mentoring cross-functional teams of engineers and analysts to build models for multiple large portfolios.
